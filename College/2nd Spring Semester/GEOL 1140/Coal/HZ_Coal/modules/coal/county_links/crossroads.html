Crossroads is one of the nicest towns in the area. Located at the intersection of Routes 857 and 226, it is the business center for the many farms and orchards that have been developed on the fertile soils of the Chambersburg Formation to the north and west of the town. Some wealth was brought to the Crossroads when natural gas was discovered in the Twin Fork Gas Field in the late 1970's. The town was devastated by an earthquake on the Twin Fork Fault in 1897. Population: 3221.
