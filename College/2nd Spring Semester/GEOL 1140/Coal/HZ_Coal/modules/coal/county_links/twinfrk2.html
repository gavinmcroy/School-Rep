The Twin Fork Fault is a northwest-southeast trending right-lateral fault located about 25 miles northeast of Hazard City. No living resident of Hazard City has ever felt an earthquake produced by this fault. The only recorded activity along this fault occurred in 1897, when a major earthquake generated MMI VIII effects within Hazard City. The same earthquake leveled every building in the town of Crossroads, which is located only 9 miles from the hypothesized epicenter. A few aftershocks were felt over the next few years; however, they were all less than MMI IV. More detailed information is not available because the first seismographs in this area were not installed until the 1950's.
