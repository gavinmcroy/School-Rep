Site Assessment

Assessment of a shoreline property begins with a knowledge of shoreline processes and how they can alter the shoreline environment. Other assessment resources include:

a) Geology and oceanography textbooks

b) Online resources such as the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers

c) Case studies of shorelines with similar environments

d) Maps and photos that show historic shoreline positions and geometries

e) Recollections of long-time residents

f) News articles and proposed public works projects

Your job is to investigate the four properties shown as "Site 1" through "Site 4" on the navigation bar. For each property you are to determine if shoreline erosion is a potential problem in the foreseeable future and report the information on the property to your client. You might want to click the "Your Report" button to determine exactly what you need to do to complete this assignment.
