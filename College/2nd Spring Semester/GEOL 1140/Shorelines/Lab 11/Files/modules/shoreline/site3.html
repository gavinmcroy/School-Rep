Site Three

Site Three is along the Muskett River, approximately one mile upstream from its mouth. Here the Muskett is meandering with point bar deposits and cut banks. No roads enter this swampy area and your real estate client has two properties in mind for constructing a cottage. These are labeled "1" and "2" on the map. He intends to build a boat dock on the river bank to provide easy access to the cottage by boat.

Your job is to determine if either of these properties is at risk from streambank erosion problems.

  


Muskett River - Flowing towards the northeast.
