Site One

Your first properties for evaluation are along Shoreline Drive. When you arrive you see a construction crane placing rip-rap on a groin. Local newspapers report that a local government contract has been awarded for building a series of groins along the shoreline. The last three groins in the sequence will be in the Shoreline Drive area. Their locations are shown on the map below.

You are to report on the potential for erosion problems at the Simpson and Klaster properties. These properties are currently for sale and your real estate investor client in Hazard City has expressed an interest in them.

You talk to the construction foreman who tells you that strong winds from the east produce waves that have been eroding the beach at an average rate of about five feet per year. He also tells you that these three groins are the last that will be built in this area.
